Caramel Macchiato:
Starbucks

This is one of Starbucks most popular espresso drinks. It's a sweet caramel drink made with a delicious vanilla syrup that is swirled into a rich and intense shot of espresso. The drink is then finished off with steamed milk and a beautiful whip of caramel drizzle.

Almond Milk Honey Flat White:
Summer Collection

This drink uses the classic espresso and almond milk (instead of dairy) and adds a touch of honey for sweetness, making it a perfect choice for those who are vegan or have lactose intolerance. With its creamy, nutty sweetness, this Flat White is sure to please coffee lovers of all kinds.

Pumpkin Spice Latte:
It's Starbucks

This drink is made with espresso, milk, and pumpkin spice sauce comprising classic fall flavors of pumpkin, cinnamon, nutmeg, and clove. The cup is topped off with whipped cream and real pumpkin pie spices.

Caffè Mocha:
Summer Collection

If you're a fan of coffee with a deep chocolate undertone, you may enjoy a cup of Caffe Mocha, which is essentially a lovechild of espresso and chocolate. The Starbucks Caffe Mocha is made with its signature intense, full-bodied, and caffeine-charged espresso, dark mocha sauce, and creamy milk. The cup is then topped off with sweetened whipped.
